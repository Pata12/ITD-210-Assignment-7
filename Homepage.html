Information about the Makerspace:
The college's new makerspace, located on the Virginia Beach Campus, invites all TCC students to collaborate on projects and ideas across various disciplines, including engineering, electronics, IT, English and history. The space houses 3-D printers, computers, laser cutters, large electronic stations and vinyl cutters.
